# Demented Horror Watchlist (2024-2026)

Curated list of the most unhinged, disturbing, and depraved horror movies available on [Dopebox](https://dopebox.to/genre/horror).

GitHub: https://github.com/disp-leg/demented-horror-watchlist

Last updated: March 28, 2026

## User Preferences

- Prefers no found footage
- Likes high pace, polished production
- Loves both extreme gore/splatter AND psychological dread
- Favorite lane: humans doing sick things to other humans (captivity, torture, psychological games)

---

## TIER 1 — Genuinely Sick (humans doing awful things to humans)

### [Cannibal Mukbang](https://dopebox.to/search/cannibal%20mukbang) (2025)
- **Vibe:** Cannibal romance / body horror
- **Synopsis:** Romance turns cannibal. Sex, mukbang livestreams, and eating people. Tight indie with queasy charm. Compared to early Stuart Gordon.
- **Demented factor:** █████████░ 9/10

### [Takeout](https://dopebox.to/search/takeout) (2025)
- **Vibe:** Serial killer / slasher
- **Synopsis:** Night shift diner workers discover a customer is a serial killer. Tense, gory, effective. Comedic beats but doesn't pull punches on kills.
- **Demented factor:** ████████░░ 8/10

### [Dinner to Die For](https://dopebox.to/search/dinner%20to%20die%20for) (2025)
- **Vibe:** Gastro-horror / true crime obsession
- **Synopsis:** True crime-obsessed couple invites neighbor to a killer role-play dinner party. Boundaries collapse. Slow burn with a killer payoff.
- **Demented factor:** ████████░░ 8/10

### [Meat Kills](https://dopebox.to/search/meat%20kills) (2025)
- **Vibe:** Extreme horror / gore
- **Synopsis:** Animal rights activists invade a pig farm with extreme ideas. Violence spirals completely out of control. Genuinely depraved.
- **Demented factor:** █████████░ 9/10

### [Stream](https://dopebox.to/search/stream) (2024)
- **Vibe:** Sadistic slasher / dark web
- **Synopsis:** Hotel guests stalked by 4 masked killers, streamed live on the dark web for gambling sickos. Terrifier team's gore FX. Tim Curry, Tony Todd, Jeffrey Combs.
- **Demented factor:** █████████░ 9/10

### [Dark Match](https://dopebox.to/search/dark%20match) (2024)
- **Vibe:** Cult slasher / wrestling horror
- **Synopsis:** Indie wrestling troupe takes a gig in a backwoods cult town. 80s setting. Ultra-violent, gory kills, raw and intense. Chris Jericho stars.
- **Demented factor:** ████████░░ 8/10

## TIER 2 — Violent & Twisted (high production, fast-paced)

### [The Toxic Avenger](https://dopebox.to/search/toxic%20avenger) (2025)
- **Vibe:** Splatter / body horror / dark comedy
- **Synopsis:** Extremely gory reboot. Peter Dinklage, Kevin Bacon, Elijah Wood. Troma carnage with a real budget. Humans getting obliterated.
- **Demented factor:** ████████░░ 8/10

### [Twisted](https://dopebox.to/search/twisted) (2026)
- **Vibe:** Captivity / revenge horror
- **Synopsis:** Darren Lynn Bousman (Saw II-IV). Apartment scammers meet an owner with a hidden sadistic agenda. Stars Lauren LaVera (Terrifier).
- **Demented factor:** ████████░░ 8/10

### [Silent Night, Deadly Night](https://dopebox.to/search/silent%20night%20deadly) (2025)
- **Vibe:** Grindhouse / Christmas slasher
- **Synopsis:** Grindhouse Christmas slasher remake. Brain-busting kills, Nazi party massacre, gonzo energy.
- **Demented factor:** ████████░░ 8/10

### [Psycho Killer](https://dopebox.to/search/psycho%20killer) (2026)
- **Vibe:** Serial killer / satanic thriller
- **Synopsis:** Se7en screenwriter. Satanic serial killer, ritualistic crime scenes, pentagrams. Polished 20th Century Studios production.
- **Demented factor:** ███████░░░ 7/10

### [The Blade Cuts Deeper](https://dopebox.to/search/the%20blade%20cuts%20deeper) (2025)
- **Vibe:** Neo-giallo / slasher
- **Synopsis:** Black-gloved killer, brutal kills, stylish. Modern giallo with old-school suspense.
- **Demented factor:** ███████░░░ 7/10

### [Humane](https://dopebox.to/search/humane) (2024)
- **Vibe:** Dark satire / family horror
- **Synopsis:** Caitlin Cronenberg. Government euthanasia program forces a wealthy family to choose which sibling dies. Goes full fratricidal.
- **Demented factor:** ████████░░ 8/10

## TIER 3 — Worth a Look

### [Spread: Pigs to Slaughter](https://dopebox.to/search/spread) (2025)
- **Vibe:** Cult slasher
- **Synopsis:** Serial killer cult with cartoon pig masks. Scream-meets-Big Chill energy.
- **Demented factor:** ██████░░░░ 6/10

### [Primate](https://dopebox.to/search/primate) (2026)
- **Vibe:** Creature horror / extreme violence
- **Synopsis:** Chimps disemboweling teenagers. Practical FX. "Way gnarlier than anticipated."
- **Demented factor:** █████████░ 9/10

### [Cold Storage](https://dopebox.to/search/cold%20storage) (2026)
- **Vibe:** Horror-comedy / parasitic
- **Synopsis:** Parasitic fungus escapes a government facility into a self-storage unit. Joe Keery, Liam Neeson, Georgina Campbell.
- **Demented factor:** ██████░░░░ 6/10
